About Us

The McMaster Undergraduate Physics Society (MUPS) serves the academic and social needs of about 100 undergraduate physics students at McMaster University. We're your link to the department, we represent you within the McMaster Science Society, and we host various events throughout the year that serve to enhance your undergraduate experience, including pub nights, intramurals, and academic sessions.
